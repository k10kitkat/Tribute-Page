Tribute to Gary Fischer, AKA Henchman 21

Gary Fischer became Henchman 21, when he was abducted by The Monarch's henchman mistakenly during a class trip. The henchman were supposed to take the diplomat that Gary was meeting, but since Gary was a "big kid", he was taken instead. When The Monarch instructed them to kill Gary, they spared his life instead, and thus he became Henchman 21. For more information about Henchman 21, see this link.
